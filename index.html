Annabel Curphey

Songwriter, Artist and Music Producer

Hi! I’m Annabel, a songwriter from the Isle of Man, currently based in Brighton.

Ever since I could pick up a guitar I’ve been writing my own songs. My first hit was titled “I Like Food” and it was a banger (just ask my dad).

Now, my writing style tends to mirror the artists I’m listening to at the time. Originally I was inspired by people like Ed Sheeran and Tenacious D, now my songs are similar to Frank Ocean and Daniel Ceasar. I love exploring new genres and taking on a challenge.

If you would like to work with me or ask me a question, please feel free via the email below.
